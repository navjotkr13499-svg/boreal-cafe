Our Story

A Warm Corner
on Water Street

Nestled in the heart of downtown St. John's, Boreal Café is your sanctuary from the Atlantic chill — a warm, inviting space where great coffee meets genuine Newfoundland hospitality.

We believe in quality you can taste: locally sourced ingredients, artisan brewing techniques, and pastries baked fresh every single morning.
